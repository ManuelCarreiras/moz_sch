## 1. Introduction

### What is Santa Isabel Escola?

Santa Isabel Escola is a school management platform that helps students, teachers, secretaries, and administrators manage academic information, grades, attendance, schedules, and finances in one place.

### How to Access the Platform

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).
2. Go to the platform URL provided by your school (for example: `http://your-school-url` or `https://santa-isabel.example.com`).
3. You will see the landing page with the school logo and a **Sign In** or **Login** button.

[Screenshot: Landing page with Sign In button]

### First-Time Login

If you received a temporary password by email:

1. Log in with your email and the temporary password.
2. The system will ask you to create a new password.
3. Enter your new password twice and confirm.
4. After that, use your email and your new password to log in.

[Screenshot: Password change screen]

### Roles and What You See

Depending on your role, you will see different parts of the platform:

| Role | What you can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Student** | View grades, assignments, attendance, schedule, and resources |
| **Teacher** | Manage classes, grades, assignments, attendance, and resources |
| **Secretary** | Create students and teachers, manage guardians, academic setup, and classes |
| **Financial** | Manage student payments (mensality), teacher salaries, and staff salaries |
| **Administrator** | Full access: everything above plus staff management |

## 2. Logging In and Out

### How to Log In

1. Go to the platform URL.
2. Click **Sign In** or **Login**.
3. Enter your **email address**.
4. Enter your **password**.
5. Click **Sign In** or **Submit**.

[Screenshot: Login form]

### How to Log Out

1. Look for the **Sign Out** or **Log out** button. It is usually in the top-right corner of the screen, next to your email or name.
2. Click **Sign Out**.
3. You will be returned to the landing page.

[Screenshot: Sign Out button location]

## 3. For Students

When you log in as a student, you see the **Student Portal** with a sidebar. Use the tabs to navigate.

### Overview

- Summary of your performance.
- Class test score distribution.

[Screenshot: Student Overview tab]

### Grades

1. Click **Grades** in the sidebar.
2. Use the filters (Year, Term, Subject, Class) at the top to narrow the view.
3. You can see:
   - Year averages
   - Individual assignment grades
   - Performance metrics

[Screenshot: Student Grades tab with filters]

### Schedule

1. Click **Schedule** in the sidebar.
2. Select the **School Year** and **Term** from the dropdowns.
3. Your weekly class timetable will appear with subjects, teachers, times, and rooms.

[Screenshot: Student Schedule]

### Profile

- View and update your personal information (if this feature is available).

### Resources

1. Click **Resources** in the sidebar.
2. Use the filters to find materials by year, term, or subject.
3. Click on a resource to download it.

[Screenshot: Resources list]

### Attendance

1. Click **Attendance** in the sidebar.
2. Use the filters to see your attendance records.
3. You can see statistics and status for each class.

[Screenshot: Student Attendance records]

### Assignments

1. Click **Assignments** in the sidebar.
2. You can view:
   - List of assigned work
   - Deadlines
   - Status (pending, submitted, graded)
3. Use filters (Year, Term, Subject, Class) to find specific assignments.

[Screenshot: Student Assignments list]

## 4. For Teachers

When you log in as a teacher, you see the **Teacher Portal** with a sidebar.

### Overview

- Dashboard summary of your classes and activities.

[Screenshot: Teacher Overview]

### My Classes

1. Click **My Classes** in the sidebar.
2. View all classes assigned to you.
3. Select a class to see its schedule and details.

[Screenshot: Teacher classes list]

### Students

1. Click **Students** in the sidebar.
2. Use filters (Year, Term, Subject) to find your students.
3. View student performance metrics and grades.

[Screenshot: Teacher Students view]

### Grades (Gradebook)

1. Click **Grades** in the sidebar.
2. Select the class and assignment.
3. Enter or update grades for each student.
4. The system calculates weighted averages and year grades automatically.

[Screenshot: Gradebook with grade entry]

### Resources

1. Click **Resources** in the sidebar.
2. Upload new resources (files, links).
3. Manage existing resources.

[Screenshot: Teacher Resources]

### Attendance

1. Click **Attendance** in the sidebar.
2. Select the class and date.
3. Mark each student as Present, Absent, Late, or Excused.
4. Use bulk actions to mark the whole class at once.
5. Click **Save** when done.

[Screenshot: Attendance taking interface]

### Assignments

1. Click **Assignments** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Create Assignment** to add a new one.
3. Fill in: title, description, due date, subject, class.
4. **Publish** the assignment when it is ready for students to see.
5. Use filters to manage existing assignments.

[Screenshot: Create Assignment form]

## 5. For Secretaries

When you log in as a secretary, you see the **Dashboard** (or Secretary Portal) with these tabs: Overview, Students, Teachers, Guardian Management, Academic Setup, Academic Foundation, and Classes.

### Overview

- Quick access to student and teacher information.
- Use the dropdowns to select School Year, Class, and then a Student or Teacher.

[Screenshot: Secretary Overview with dropdowns]

### Students

1. Click **Students** in the sidebar.
2. You will be taken to the Student Portal view.
3. Click **Create Student** to add a new student.
4. Fill in the required fields (name, email, date of birth, etc.).
5. The system creates a login account for the student automatically.

[Screenshot: Create Student form]

### Teachers

1. Click **Teachers** in the sidebar.
2. You will be taken to the Teacher Portal view.
3. Click **Create Teacher** to add a new teacher.
4. Fill in the required fields (name, email, phone, etc.).
5. The system creates a login account for the teacher automatically.

[Screenshot: Create Teacher form]

### Guardian Management

1. Click **Guardian Management** in the sidebar.
2. You can:
   - **Create New Guardian**: Add a guardian with contact details.
   - **Assign Guardian to Student**: Link guardians to students and choose the relationship type (Parent, Grandparent, etc.).

[Screenshot: Guardian Management options]

### Academic Setup

1. Click **Academic Setup** in the sidebar.
2. You can manage:
   - **Departments**: Organize subjects (e.g., Mathematics, Science).
   - **Subjects & Score Ranges**: Define subjects and grading scales.
   - **Classrooms**: Manage rooms and capacity.
   - **Teacher Department Assignment**: Assign teachers to departments.
   - **Grading Criteria**: Set how grades are calculated (tests, homework, attendance).
   - **Assessment Types**: Define assignment types (Homework, Quiz, Test, Project).
   - **School Year Management**: Manage school years and structure.

[Screenshot: Academic Setup menu]

### Academic Foundation

- Manage year levels, terms, school years, and student assignments to year levels.

[Screenshot: Academic Foundation]

### Classes

1. Click **Classes** in the sidebar.
2. View and manage classes.
3. **Enrollments**: Assign students to classes.
4. **Timetable**: Set or view the class schedule.

[Screenshot: Classes and Enrollments]

## 6. For Financial Users

When you log in as a financial user, you see the **Financial Portal** with one main tab: **Financial Management**. Inside it there are three sub-tabs: Mensality, Teacher Salary, and Staff Salary.

### Mensality (Student Monthly Payments)

1. Click **Financial Management** in the sidebar.
2. Select the **Mensality** tab.
3. Use the filters:
   - **Month** and **Year**: See payments for a specific period.
   - **Student**: Filter by student.
   - **Paid/Unpaid**: See only paid or unpaid records.
4. To create a new payment: Click **Create** or **Add**, fill in student, value, due date, and month/year.
5. To generate payments for all students: Click **Generate**, select month, year, and due date.
6. To mark as paid: Find the record and change the status to **Paid**, optionally adding the payment date.

[Screenshot: Mensality tab with filters]

### Teacher Salary

1. Click the **Teacher Salary** sub-tab.
2. Use the same filters (Month, Year, Teacher, Paid/Unpaid).
3. To set base salaries: Click **Salary Grid**, then edit the base salary for each teacher.
4. To generate monthly salaries: Click **Generate**, select month, year, and due date.
5. To mark as paid: Update the record and set **Paid** to Yes.

[Screenshot: Teacher Salary tab]

### Staff Salary

1. Click the **Staff Salary** sub-tab.
2. Use filters (Month, Year, Staff, Paid/Unpaid).
3. To set base salaries: Click **Staff Salary Grid**, then edit the base salary for each staff member.
4. To generate monthly salaries: Click **Generate**, select month, year, and due date.
5. To mark as paid: Update the record and set **Paid** to Yes.

[Screenshot: Staff Salary tab]

## 8. Common Tasks (Quick Reference)

### Changing Filters

Many screens use **cascading filters**:

1. Select **School Year** first.
2. Then select **Term** (options update based on the year).
3. Then **Subject** or **Class** if needed.
4. When you change the year, other filters may reset.

### Where to Find Sign Out

- **Student Portal**: Top-right, next to your name or email.
- **Teacher Portal**: Top-right, next to your name or email.
- **Dashboard (Admin/Secretary/Financial)**: Top-right, next to "Welcome, [your email]".

## 9. Troubleshooting

### I Cannot Log In

- **Wrong password**: Make sure you use the correct password. If you forgot it, use "Forgot password" if available, or ask an administrator to reset it.
- **Account not created**: If you are a new student or teacher, your account may not be created yet. Ask the school secretary or administrator.

### I Don't See Expected Data

- **Check filters**: Make sure the correct Year, Term, Subject, or Class is selected. Try "All" or reset the filters.
- **Missing records**: Some data (e.g., grades, schedules) must be added by teachers or administrators. Ask them to add it if it is missing.

### Something Looks Wrong

- **Refresh the page**: Press F5 or click the refresh button in your browser.
- **Try another browser**: If the page does not load correctly, try Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

### Who to Contact

- **Students**: Contact your teacher or the school secretary.
- **Teachers**: Contact the school secretary or administrator.
- **Secretaries / Financial users**: Contact the school administrator or IT support.
